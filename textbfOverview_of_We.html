Overview of Well-Architected reviews
Well-Architected Reviews are a critical component of maintaining and enhancing cloud architectures using AWS. These reviews are detailed evaluations conducted by AWS-certified architects or trained professionals within an organization. The goal is to assess the alignment of cloud deployments with the best practices and principles outlined in the AWS Well-Architected Framework, covering all five pillars: Operational Excellence, Security, Reliability, Performance Efficiency, and Cost Optimization.
