Architectural patterns and services
High availability and fault tolerance are about designing systems that are always operational and can withstand and recover from failures. Essential strategies include:
Multi-AZ and multi-region deployments : Deploy applications across multiple Availability Zones and even regions to ensure service continuity in case of a zone or regional failure.
Backup and disaster recovery : Use Amazon RDS which automatically takes snapshots and backups to streamline recovery processes. Implement AWS Backup to centrally manage backups across AWS services.
Use case : A financial services firm uses Amazon RDS with Multi-AZ deployments for its transaction databases to ensure seamless failover to a standby instance if the primary instance fails. They also deploy their application stack in multiple regions to provide service continuity in case of regional disruptions.
